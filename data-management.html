##Backing up data

One of the key principles of the Guerrilla Analytics workflow is frequently and properly making back-ups of all data that is associated with a project. Generally speaking, best practice for this is making use of online cloud storage since these services often provide data redundancy (multiple data centers spread across the globe containing copies of the same data). However, in the case of working with extremely sensitive or confidential data where there is a high risk of data theft, cloud storage might not always be an option. In those situations where local storage is preferred, a RAID 1 system can be used. This now often forgotten technology, which was widely used in the 2000’s, makes use of two storage units A and B where unit B contains an 1:1 copy of unit A.
